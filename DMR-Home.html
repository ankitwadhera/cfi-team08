    Features

    Digital Medical Reconciliation (DMR) is a framework to link various hospitals, doctors across the country for OTP based registration and appointment system where patient received a unique OTP after registration which is used for further appointment. We developed this portal in thinking of rural areas so that proper medication, diagnosis, services that can be provided to the patient immediately. This portal is currently running on a local host database. In future we will use cloud services for storing data. Portal facilitates online appointments with various departments of different Hospitals using their mobile number. The system will take care of the appointments of doctors and will have facility to get next patient in case the appointee is not available or absent. Doctors will be able feed the prescription via keystrokes, file or cam scanner to the repository. This system will automatically generate notification and send to the individual on their phone for pending medical checkups, medications, medical test and treatments.

    • Simple appointment process

      We are making the process simpler. Patient has his registration details. All he has to do is to verify his registration details which he has used at the time of registration, verify his mobile number, select hospital, department, select date of appointment and receive confirmation for appointment

    • Dashboard Reports

      Dashboard has reports for blood banks .Dashboard with the details of medicines being utilized , disease type, type of medication provided area wise .Printing the reports or take away on email

    • Hospital On Boarding

      Doctors will be able feed the prescription via keystrokes, file or cam scanner to the repository. This system will automatically generate notification and send to the individual on their phone for pending medical checkups, medications, medical test and treatments. Hospitals can come on board this platform and provide their appointment slots for online booking by patients. The system facilitates Hospitals to easily manage their registration and appointment process and monitor the flow of patients.
